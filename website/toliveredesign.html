Wireframes

Wireframing played a key role in aligning with the client on the overall structure before diving into details. This process helped define the sitemap and establish a clear, intuitive URL structure ensuring both, business objectives and user needs were aligned from the start.

Navigation

To Live is a non-profit organization that manages 3 different venues to support artist and art events across Toronto. Their original navigation was extensive and had plenty of duplicates. The amalgamation and simplification of their navigation were one of the main challenges.

When searching in a wide set of categories, types of events, artists names, simplifying the search process was a crucial feature that directly impacted their front line business, selling tickets and booking events/venues.

Friendly Prediction + passive suggestions when searching were meant to speed up the search process giving the high amount of options the user may face when searching for a particular category.

Incorporating date range selection along with pre-defined options like "today" or "tomorrow" expedites search flow by swiftly narrowing down choices.

Implementing tailored filters based on TOLive's data analysis improves search accuracy, saving time and enhancing precision for users.

Layout Flexibility

Responsivenes and minimalistic design allow this website to display a structure that is customizable throught a headless CMS to innovate when creating new pages for artist, events, podcasts or stories while maintaing the asthetics and consistency in visual terms.
